# Shemo Studio Tone of Voice

> Reference for stations 16-20. This guide keeps Arabic and English content aligned with the approved brand direction: Hybrid studio + visible founder.

## Core Position

Shemo Studio is a boutique creative studio with a visible founder. The voice should feel personal enough to build trust, but structured enough to feel like a serious studio.

Arabic is the primary launch language. English should be a natural counterpart, not a word-for-word shadow.

## Voice Pillars

| Pillar | Arabic Direction | English Direction |
|---|---|---|
| Clear craft | اشرح الشغل كعملية مفهومة: فكرة، اسكتش، تنفيذ، تسليم. | Explain the work as a clear process: idea, sketch, production, delivery. |
| Honest confidence | واثق من غير مبالغة أو وعود ضخمة. | Confident without hype or oversized promises. |
| Founder-led | يظهر حضور شيمو كقائد إبداعي، مع مساحة لنمو الاستوديو كفريق. | Let Shemo feel present as creative lead, while leaving room for the studio to grow. |
| Visual thinking | استخدم لغة الصورة والحركة والتحويل من الاسكتش للشاشة. | Use visual language: frames, motion, sketches, scenes, and transformation. |
| Practical warmth | ودود ومباشر، مش رسمي زيادة ولا عامي زيادة. | Warm and direct, never stiff and never too casual. |

## Arabic Style

- استخدم عربية فصحى سهلة بلمسة مصرية خفيفة عند الحاجة، بدون عامية ثقيلة.
- خاطب العميل بصيغة محترمة ومباشرة: "نحوّل فكرتك"، "نرتّب الرسالة"، "نصمّم مشهدًا واضحًا".
- تجنب المبالغات مثل: "الأفضل في السوق"، "نتائج مضمونة"، "ثورة في البراند".
- لا تدّعي وجود عملاء أو نتائج غير حقيقية.
- عند ذكر مشاريع Demo/Concept، يجب وسمها بوضوح: "مشروع تجريبي"، "Concept"، "غير منفّذ لعميل تجاري".

## English Style

- Use concise, polished English. Avoid agency buzzwords and inflated claims.
- Prefer concrete verbs: shape, design, edit, build, refine, deliver.
- Keep the founder visible without turning the site into a personal diary.
- Do not imply paid client work when the piece is demo, concept, internal, student, or personal.
- Label demo work clearly: "Demo / Concept Project", "Not commissioned by a client", or "Internal creative study".

## Bilingual Rules

- Arabic and English pages should carry the same promise, but each language may use natural phrasing.
- Arabic is the source of direction when there is tension between versions.
- Do not auto-translate idioms literally.
- CTAs should stay short and action-led:
  - Arabic: "ابدأ مشروعك", "اطلب عرض سعر", "شاهد العمل"
  - English: "Start a Project", "Request a Quote", "View Work"
- Service names may keep English terms when they are industry-standard, but explain them in Arabic.

## Case Study Rules

Until real client projects are available, Work and Case Studies may use clearly labeled Demo/Concept Projects.

Required label near the title and in metadata:

- Arabic: "مشروع تجريبي / Concept - غير منفّذ لعميل تجاري"
- English: "Demo / Concept Project - Not commissioned by a client"

Allowed project types:

- Concept service showcase.
- Internal creative study.
- Personal or experimental piece.
- Reconstructed workflow sample using original assets made for the site.

Not allowed:

- Fake client names.
- Fake metrics or testimonials.
- Logos or brand names that imply a real engagement.
- Before/after claims unless the before and after were both created for the demo.

## Page-Level Notes

| Page Area | Tone |
|---|---|
| Home | Crisp, cinematic, trust-building. Establish the studio promise quickly. |
| About | Founder-visible, grounded, specific. Avoid over-mythologizing. |
| Services | Practical and outcome-focused. Show what the client gets. |
| Work | Transparent about demo/concept status until real client work exists. |
| Packages | Clear scope, expectations, and revision boundaries. |
| Forms | Calm, helpful, low-friction. Ask only what is needed. |
| Policies | Plain, precise, and fair. No legal fog. |

## Sample Microcopy

Arabic:

- "من اسكتش أولي إلى مشهد جاهز للنشر."
- "نرتّب الفكرة، نبني اتجاهًا بصريًا، ونحوّلها إلى محتوى واضح."
- "هذا مشروع تجريبي يوضح طريقة التفكير والتنفيذ، وليس عملًا منفّذًا لعميل تجاري."

English:

- "From first sketch to publish-ready screen."
- "We shape the idea, define the visual direction, and turn it into clear creative output."
- "This is a demo concept showing the workflow and craft, not commissioned client work."
